The Organization

LoL Esports at NC State was originally formed in August 2015, and the organization was officially created in March 2018. The purpose of the organization is to foster the optimal environment at NC State to grow the best collegiate League of Legends team. Over the past four years, the organization has earned $68,000 in scholarship money and made various accomplishments in both the amateur and collegiate scenes. In addition, our Varsity roster in the 2018-2019 school year qualified for the Collegiate Championship and represented the South as one of the top eight teams in North America. We are excited to continue this success and strive higher in the years to come!

Check out our Gamepedia page for more information about our acheivements.
